ACME CORPORATION - EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK
=====================================

SECTION 1: TIME OFF POLICY

1.1 Paid Time Off (PTO)
All full-time employees receive 15 days of PTO per year, accruing at 1.25 days per month.
PTO can be used for vacation, personal days, or sick leave.
Unused PTO carries over up to a maximum of 5 days per year.

1.2 Holidays
The company observes the following paid holidays:
- New Year's Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving Day
- Christmas Day

1.3 Requesting Time Off
Submit PTO requests through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance.
Emergency requests will be considered on a case-by-case basis.
Manager approval is required for all time off requests.

SECTION 2: REMOTE WORK POLICY

2.1 Eligibility
Employees in good standing who have completed 90 days of employment are eligible for remote work.
Some positions require on-site presence and may not be eligible.

2.2 Equipment
The company provides a laptop and monitor for remote workers.
Employees are responsible for maintaining a stable internet connection.
IT support is available Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM EST.

2.3 Expectations
Remote employees must be available during core hours (10 AM - 3 PM in their timezone).
Video should be on during team meetings.
Response time to messages should not exceed 2 hours during work hours.

SECTION 3: EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT

3.1 Eligible Expenses
- Business travel (flights, hotels, ground transportation)
- Client meals (up to $75 per person)
- Professional development (pre-approved courses and conferences)
- Home office equipment (up to $500 annually, pre-approved)

3.2 Submission Process
Submit expense reports within 30 days of the expense.
Include itemized receipts for all expenses over $25.
Reimbursement is processed within 2 pay periods.

3.3 Travel Policy
Book travel at least 14 days in advance when possible.
Use economy class for flights under 4 hours.
Daily meal allowance: $75 domestic, $100 international.

SECTION 4: REFUND POLICY

4.1 Customer Refunds
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days for all purchases made within 30 days.
Original payment method will be credited.
Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

4.2 Return Process
Contact customer service to initiate a return.
Products must be in original packaging.
A prepaid shipping label will be provided for defective items.
